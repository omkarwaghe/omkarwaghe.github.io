Flow and Flow Accelarators in IC Agile

Achieving flow in Agile

  1. Smooth work progress with minimal bottlenecks
  2. Continous value delivery
  3. Clear priorities and next step for everyone
  4. Reduced waste and rework
  5. Improved predictablity and reliability

Flow accelarators are techniques or tools that remove workflow obstacles and boost efficiency
A common exaple is limiting work in progress to help maintain focus and steady progress.

Most Powerful accelarators

  1. Limiting WIP - Limiting task and using visual tools like kanban boards improves focus, reduces delays and enhances transperancy team focus on fewer items
  2. Visual Management - Visualize Agile Workdlows with Regular feedback loops
    • See the workflow
    • Identify bottlenecks early
    • Track progress at glance
    • improve accountability and flow
  3. Frequent Feedback Loops - Regular pause to inspect and adjust
    • Daily scrums and Stand-ups
    • Team Syncs
    • Retrospective
    • System demos
    • Backlog refinements
    • Customer or stakeholder reviews
  4. Automation for Repititive work
    • Repetitive work shows flow
    • Automate testing, Deployment, reporting and checks
    • Eliminate manual effort
    • Significantly speed up delivery
  5. Reducing Batch Sizes
    • Large batches slow flow and delay feedback
